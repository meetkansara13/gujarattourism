About Gujarat Tourism

Preserving culture, promoting exploration — and redefining how the world experiences Gujarat.

Our Vision

To position Gujarat as one of the world’s most culturally rich, safe and globally admired travel destinations – while ensuring sustainable tourism and preserving our heritage for the next generations.

Our Mission

We work to highlight Gujarat’s diverse landscapes — the White Desert of Kutch, historic cities, sacred temples, wildlife, and coastal trails — while offering modern tourism facilities, digital empowerment, and seamless travel experiences.

Who We Are

Gujarat Tourism is a government-led initiative committed to promoting the state's art, architecture, culture, festivals, handicrafts, and hidden gems. Our team includes storytellers, historians, digital innovators, cultural experts, and tourism specialists.
